\noindent Properties / Rules of logarithms:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Product: $\log_b m \times n = \log_b m + log_b n$
    \item Quotient: $\log_b \frac{m}{n} = \log_b m - log_b n$
    \item Power: $\log_bm^p = p \times \log_b m$
\end{itemize}
